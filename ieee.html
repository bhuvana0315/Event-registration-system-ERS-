About IEEE



IEEE is the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe. Membership IEEE membership offers access to technical innovation, cutting-edge information, networking opportunities, and exclusive member benefits. The world's largest professional association for the advancement of technology. Student Branches provide an opportunity for IEEE Student members to begin networking in their areas of interest and future profession. There are over 2,000 Student Branches in over 100 countries, globally. See below for a Branch near you!


The following are the events conducted by IEEE: 1) Bleep  2) Educational bingo  3) Bootcamps on Ethical hacking

All participants will get participation e-certificates.

Bootcamps on EH



It is a 5-day bootcamp sessions. Ethical hacking bootcamps are intensive classes designed to quickly teach students the fundamentals of ethical hacking. They provide hands-on experience in a simulated environment.



Educational bingo




It is puzzle type of game which consists of incomplete words. Hints will be given and you can fill in the missing letters.
Top performers will be awarded with exciting prizes.



Bleep



You need to pick the topic from the Bowl of technical topics and speak about it for 1 to 2 mins the twist is that you cannot mention your topic throughout your speech.
Top performers will be awarded with exciting prizes.
