There are a number of benefits to leading a GDSC chapter, but here are a few that stand out:
1)Professional growth - Access to community management training and technical knowledge to help you be a stronger leader, and receive invitations to select Google events.
2)Network growth - Access to a global network of student leaders, professional community organizers, industry experts, and Googlers to gain mentorship and share knowledge.

Web Development




It is a group competition.The group should contain not less than 5 members.
This competition involves three rounds.
1.Idea Submission(Online)
2.Powerpoint Presentation(Online)
3.Project Submission(Offline)
Problem statement:You are supposed to identify problems in your society and develop solution to the problem identified.You can use any type of framework and web technologies.The project has to be done in the given time.

Technical Quiz




This is a tech based quiz.There will be three rounds(easy,medium,hard).In first round there will be 30 easy questions to be answered in 15mins. In second round there will be 15 questions to be answered in 15mins.In final round there will be 5 questions for 15mins. Topics includes latest technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning.Along with questions related to Python, C, C++, Java,Operating Systems, Computer Networks(as per the year of study)
